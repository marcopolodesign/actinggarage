# Course Enrollment Growth Strategy Analysis
## TAG Acting School - Action Plan to Increase Student Sign-ups

**Analysis Date:** December 2024  
**Total Current Students:** 328  
**Current Enrollment Distribution:** 577 course enrollments

---

## 📊 Current Performance Overview

### Top Performing Courses (by enrollment)

1. **INTENSIVOS 24 25** - 38 students (11.6%) 🏆
2. **PRO 4 I (un día) MARTES a parti** - 21 students (6.4%)
3. **PRO 1O I L - X** - 16 students (4.9%)
4. **PRO 10 II + PRO 4 II + ASIGNATU** - 15 students (4.6%)
5. **PRE JUVENIL JUEVES 18h** - 15 students (4.6%)

### Key Insights

- **Intensive/short-term courses** show highest appeal (INTENSIVOS leading)
- **Youth programs** (PRE JUVENIL, PRO MENORES) have strong enrollment
- **PRO programs** (PRO 4 I, PRO 10) are popular among committed students
- **Average 1.76 courses per student** indicates cross-selling potential
- **96.3% email coverage** provides excellent communication channel

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations

### 1. LEVERAGE TOP PERFORMERS (Quick Wins)

#### A. Expand Intensive Course Offerings
**Current:** INTENSIVOS 24 25 has 38 students (top performer)

**Actions:**
- ✅ Create **seasonal intensive programs** (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter)
- ✅ Offer **weekend intensives** for working professionals
- ✅ Develop **theme-based intensives** (Comedy, Drama, Film Acting, etc.)
- ✅ Create **beginner-friendly intensive** series ("Acting Bootcamp")

**Expected Impact:** 
- Intensive format already proven successful
- Lower commitment barrier = higher conversion
- Can target new demographics (weekend workers, tourists)

**Timeline:** Launch next quarter

---

#### B. Amplify PRO Program Marketing
**Current:** Multiple PRO programs showing strong performance

**Actions:**
- ✅ Create **PRO program testimonial campaign** featuring successful alumni
- ✅ Offer **trial/audition sessions** before full PRO commitment
- ✅ Develop **PRO program comparison guide** to help students choose
- ✅ Implement **early bird pricing** for PRO programs
- ✅ Create **PRO alumni network** events to build community

**Expected Impact:**
- PRO programs have committed, engaged students
- Higher retention and referral potential
- Premium positioning = higher revenue per student

**Timeline:** Next 2 months

### 2. TARGET HIGH-POTENTIAL SEGMENTS

#### A. Youth Programs (Ages 8-17)
**Current:** PRE JUVENIL courses show strong enrollment (15+ students each)

**Actions:**
- ✅ **Parent-focused marketing** - emphasize life skills, confidence building
- ✅ **School partnerships** - offer workshops to local schools
- ✅ **Summer camps** - expand CASAL offerings (currently low enrollment)
- ✅ **Sibling discounts** - encourage multiple enrollments
- ✅ **Performance showcases** - regular shows for parents (quarterly)
- ✅ **Social media content** - showcase student work (with permissions)

**Expected Impact:**
- Youth programs = longer student lifecycle
- Parents value structured extracurricular activities
- Word-of-mouth potential is high in parent communities

**Timeline:** Begin immediately

---

#### B. Adult Beginners Segment
**Current:** INT BASICO courses have 8-9 students each (opportunity to grow)

**Actions:**
- ✅ **"Never Acted Before?" campaign** - remove intimidation barrier
- ✅ **Free trial classes** - first session free for new students
- ✅ **Beginner-friendly course descriptions** - avoid jargon
- ✅ **Evening and weekend options** - accommodate work schedules
- ✅ **Corporate workshops** - team building sessions for companies
- ✅ **50+ programs** - specialized courses for mature adults

**Expected Impact:**
- Low barriers to entry
- Large addressable market
- Adults have disposable income
- Can convert to PRO programs later

**Timeline:** Launch in Q1

### 3. OPTIMIZE ENROLLMENT FUNNEL

#### A. Email Marketing Campaigns
**Current:** 316 students (96.3%) have email addresses

**Actions:**
- ✅ **Segmented campaigns:**
  - Current students → Cross-sell additional courses
  - Past students → Re-engagement campaigns
  - New leads → Welcome series with course recommendations
  
- ✅ **Email content:**
  - Course highlights and success stories
  - Limited-time enrollment incentives
  - Student testimonials and videos
  - Behind-the-scenes content from classes

- ✅ **Automation:**
  - Welcome series for new enrollments
  - Abandoned enrollment recovery emails
  - Course reminder emails before start dates

**Expected Impact:**
- Direct communication channel to 316 students
- Low cost, high ROI
- Can test and optimize messaging quickly

**Timeline:** Set up within 1 month
